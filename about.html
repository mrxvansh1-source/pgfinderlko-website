About PG Lucknow

The story behind a zero-brokerage PG platform for Lucknow

◆ Our Story

PG Lucknow started with a simple problem — finding a reliable, affordable PG in Lucknow usually means dealing with brokers, hidden charges, and listings that don't match reality. This platform was built to fix that: a place where landlords can list their properties directly, and students or working professionals can search, compare, and connect without a single rupee of brokerage.

◆ Meet the Founder

VM

Vansh Maurya

Founder & Developer, PG Lucknow
