Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

PG Lucknow ("we", "us", "the platform") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used when you use pgfinderlko.online.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name and email address when you sign in with Google.
  • Listing information: If you are a landlord, the property details, phone number, and photos/videos you choose to upload.
  • Enquiry information: If you are a student, the name and phone number you submit when sending an enquiry to a landlord.
  • Usage data: Basic interaction data such as favourited listings, for the purpose of showing them back to you.

How We Use Your Information

  • To connect students directly with landlords for PG enquiries.
  • To display your listings, favourites, and enquiry history in your profile.
  • To notify landlords in real time when a new enquiry is received.

Information Sharing

Your name and phone number submitted through an enquiry are shared only with the landlord of that specific listing, so they can contact you directly via WhatsApp or call. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage). While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

Advertising

This site may display ads served by Google AdSense, which may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ads Settings.

Your Choices

You can request deletion of your account data, listings, or enquiry history at any time by contacting us (see Contact page). You can also stop using the platform at any time.

Children's Privacy

This platform is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please reach out via our Contact page.
